# Visual Style Catalog

Use this catalog to widen outputs beyond realistic prestige posters. Choose a specific visual family, not a generic "artistic style."

## Live-Action And Photographic

- prestige live-action drama: real human scale, restrained light, grounded texture
- cinematic documentary: observational realism, editorial crop, no decorative propaganda
- noir photography: high contrast, hard shadows, smoke, silhouette, urban tension
- hard sci-fi key art: precise geometry, vast scale, clean light, sober technology
- natural history cinema: patient wildlife framing, environmental scale, dignity
- surreal live-action: realistic image with one impossible object or spatial contradiction

## Graphic Design And Modernist

- modernist symbol poster: one bold shape, limited palette, strong negative space
- cut-paper abstraction: flat forms, rough edges, simple metaphor, tactile paper
- constructivist diagonal composition: red/black/cream, strong geometry, political urgency
- Swiss grid poster: strict alignment, type-led hierarchy, minimal image
- Saul Bass-like symbolic design: sharp silhouette, limited palette, imperfect cut forms
- Polish poster metaphor: surreal central symbol, expressive texture, hand-rendered type
- editorial magazine cover: image as argument, clean type, strong crop

## Ink, Line, And East Asian Visual Language

- Chinese ink wash: mist, blank paper, ink density, poetic scale, seal-like accent
- modern guofeng poster: Chinese cultural material with contemporary film-poster composition, not costume-drama decoration
- minimal ink force: one stroke, road, shadow, or figure carries the whole poster
- red-black Chinese graphic cut: large red/black field, rupture, ritual, verdict, or revenge energy
- Chinese shadow theatre: silhouettes, cast shadows, screen layers, opera-stage depth, identity or power tension
- palace/gate pressure: architecture becomes hierarchy, law, family order, or political force
- Chinese material archive: rubbed stone, burned paper, ledger, seal, legal file, old map, or plaque as story surface
- contemporary Chinese urban guofeng: modern street, subway, office, apartment, surveillance, or neon signage organized by Chinese spatial rhythm and ritual color
- black-and-white line drawing: sparse contour, fragile detail, high white space
- white-line-on-black drawing: chalk/scratch tension, useful for horror or memory
- woodblock print: carved edges, flat color, handmade registration, strong rhythm
- ukiyo-e-inspired poster: flat planes, wave/cloud pattern, controlled outlines
- stele/rubbing texture: aged stone, rubbed ink, historical gravity
- gongbi-inspired detail: precise fine line, controlled color, elegant ornament

## Painting And Handmade

- oil-painting drama: visible brush, thick atmosphere, human warmth
- watercolor melancholy: transparent wash, soft edges, emotional fragility
- gouache poster: flat matte color, strong shape, hand-painted commercial feel
- charcoal study: smudged black, raw gesture, psychological focus
- pastel illustration: soft grain, quiet color, coming-of-age warmth
- fresco/mural epic: monumental figures, aged wall texture, mythic scale

## Animation, Anime, Cartoon

- anime theatrical key art: emotional sky, cinematic lighting, detailed background, title hierarchy
- Japanese animation nature poster: small human/animal, luminous landscape, poetic stillness
- Chinese animated epic: ink, cloud, mythic creature/figure, strong vertical rhythm
- adult animation drama: simplified characters, mature palette, graphic sadness
- stop-motion/clay poster: tactile miniature world, handmade imperfection, dramatic lighting
- editorial cartoon poster: simplified characters, witty metaphor, clean composition
- storybook poster: warm hand-drawn scene, gentle fantasy, not childish unless requested
- comic-panel poster: bold inking, limited panels, motion lines used sparingly

## Experimental And Abstract

- abstract data field: patterns become emotional landscape, no literal screens
- expressionist distortion: warped architecture/figure, psychological intensity
- dream collage: cut photos, paper shadows, symbolic layering, controlled mess
- typographic abstraction: title becomes the main image, letters as landscape/object
- minimal object metaphor: one object carries the story, almost no environment
- monochrome field poster: texture, scale, and title do the work
- surreal symbolic poster: impossible object, clear silhouette, restrained palette
- color field poster: one vast emotional color/texture field with a small readable anchor and strong title hierarchy
- symbol minimal poster: one blade, eye, chair, smoke column, door, flower, cage, or object carries the whole poster
- typographic architecture: title or letterforms become wall, road, stage, instrument, shadow, cut, or horizon
- constructivist plane conflict: diagonal geometry, limited palette, type locked to planes, public urgency
- Polish surreal metaphor: rough printed metaphor, body/object transformation, expressive lettering, oblique commentary
- risograph halftone field: one or two ink colors, dot texture, light/cloud/sky/memory as printed field
- theatrical void: black stage field, light portals, seats, throne, curtain planes, silhouettes, power hierarchy
- impressionistic memory field: tree, rain, grass, sea, street, or canopy as a heightened memory surface

## Retro And Genre Commercial

- Hong Kong painted one-sheet: dynamic angle, bold hand lettering, gritty color
- 1970s thriller print: warm paper, grainy montage, limited elements
- 1980s adventure poster: heroic scale, painted light, but avoid game-cover clutter
- VHS horror: rough print, harsh flash, decayed type, limited palette
- romantic comedy poster: bright but composed, character relationship geometry
- family animation poster: rounded shapes, color charm, clear hierarchy, not stock clipart
