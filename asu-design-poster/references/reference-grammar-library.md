# Reference Grammar Library

Use this to expand the asset base without copying a specific poster, painting, logo, living artist style, actor likeness, or protected character.

Extract neutral grammar only:

- composition
- color construction
- light and material
- figure/object scale
- typography relationship
- narrative function

## Popular Appeal Grammar

Use when the poster should be instantly readable:

- one dominant subject or symbol
- strong title contrast
- clear foreground/background depth
- a memorable accent color
- simple emotional promise
- no over-clever metaphor that hides the subject

Popular does not mean cheap. Keep craft, hierarchy, and restraint.
